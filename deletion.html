User Data Deletion Callback Instructions

In adherence to Meta Developer guidelines and global privacy standards, EmanBakery Online supports total user profile configuration deletion workflows for any connected custom applications.

Because our system functions strictly as a stateless, transient webhook routing gateway, we do not collect or archive permanent user personal records, contact rosters, or historic chat metadata. As a result, there is no permanent tracking database to clear.

However, if you wish to completely disconnect your Meta profile or communication assets from our internal system tunnels, please follow these steps:

  1. Navigate directly to your Facebook Profile settings panel and click on Apps and Websites.
  2. Locate the application titled WB (or matching our App ID sequence).
  3. Click the Remove option button positioned next to the application entry.
  4. To request immediate manual verification that your phone number mapping is cleared from our active transient memory routers, please email a formal removal request directly to: info@emanbakery.online.

Requests are processed administratively by our systems manager within 48 business hours.
